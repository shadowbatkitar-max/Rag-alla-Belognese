Ragù alla Bolognese

Ingredients

  • Pasta (best choice Tagliatelle)
  • 600g Ground Beef
  • 100g Guanciale
  • 800g Peeled Tomatoes
  • 200ml Milk 5% rich
  • 2 Big Onions
  • 2 Big Carrots
  • 3 Celery stalks
  • A half Zucchini
  • A half eggplant
  • 3 cloves of Garlic
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 spoon of Tomato paste
  • 250ml Red Wine (Dornenfelder)
  • Spices and herbs, Salt, Pepper, Oregano, Thyme, Rosemary

Instructions

  1. First rendering heat the guanciale in a bit olive oil under middle heat.
  2. Next put the finely chopped onions, carrots and celery in the pot and braise it, till roasting aromas develop.
  3. Then do the same with the chopped zucchini and eggplate
  4. Put the ground beef in the pot and rost it, till it gets a nice color.
  5. Important you put the finely chopped garlic in the pot and roast it a little bit.
  6. Next pour the red wine in the pot and wait till you hear it roasting, because we just want the aroma of the wine in the dish.
  7. Finally give the peeled tomatoes in the pot and smash them. In addtion add the spices and then keep it boiling under low heat for at least 3 hours.
  8. After 1 hour add the milk and let it boil under low heat for the nest 2 hours.
  9. Now you use a secound pot and boil the pasta aldente, you take them out and put the pasta in a pan with some of the Bolognese and boil it ben cotton in it. Whereby the Bolognese will stick on the pasta and it tastes much better.
  10. Lastly, you put the pasta on a plate and give 1 ladle of sauce over it with a little bit parmigiano reggiano. Bon appétit
